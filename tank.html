Compression Weigh Modules

Compression weight modules are devices used to measure the weight of an object by applying pressure or force. These modules are commonly used in industrial settings where accurate weight measurements are crucial for quality control and safety purposes.

The modules consist of a load cell, which is a device that converts force into an electrical signal, and a housing that protects the load cell from external factors such as moisture and dust. The load cell is typically made of metal and is designed to withstand high levels of pressure without deforming or breaking.

Compression weight modules are available in various sizes and weight capacities to accommodate different applications. They can be used in a variety of industries, including agriculture, construction, and transportation.
